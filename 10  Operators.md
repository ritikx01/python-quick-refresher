## Arithematic Operators
1. `a+b`										# Adds 'a' and 'b'
	Eg: 3+2=5
2. `a-b`										# Subtracts 'b' from 'a'
	Eg: 3-2=1
3. `a*b`										# Multiplies 'a' and 'b'
	Eg: 3\*2=6
4. `a/b`										# Divides 'a' by 'b'
	Eg: 
	1. 3/2=1.5
	2. 13/3=4.333333333333333
	3. 4/2=2.0
5. `a**b`										# Elevates 'a' to the power of 'b'
	Eg:
	1. 3*\*2 = 3<sup>2</sup>=9
